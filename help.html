How to Use the Pain Indicator

A step-by-step walkthrough on how to use the Pain Indicator tool.


Step 1

Choose the body type that best fits you. Once you have found your best fit, click on the select button below the model. Once the model is selected, the select button will turn blue.


Step 2

Enter your email address, which will allow your doctor to contact you later. Once you have done so, click on the "Go to Form" button, which will redirect you to the pain indicator tool.


Step 3

In the tool, you are able to select 3 views of the body: Overview, Muscular, and Skeletal.


Select the layer you would like to view.


Step 4

On the 3D model, you can rotate and select where you are feeling pain. The red circles indicate locations you can select. Once selected, the region will turn green.


You can also select the region where you are feeling pain in the drop-down menu.


If you would like to reset the view of the model, you can click on the reset camera button.


Step 5

After you have selected a region, you can indicate the pain level from 1 to 10 using the related slider.


You can also indicate the sharpness of your pain from 1 to 10 using the corresponding slider.


Next, you can describe your symptoms in the the provided text box.


You can then describe how long you have been having your symptoms and pain in the provided text box.


You can view all of the information you have inputted into the tool by selecting the "View Form Data" button.


When you select the "View Form Data" button, a summary will pop up with all of your information listed.


If you want to clear the form and start over, you can select the "Clear Form" button. The model and form will be reset.


Once you have completely filled out your form, you can select the "Downloard Form" button.


When you select the "Download Form" button, a .json file will be downloaded, as shown below.


You can then present your file to your doctor or primary care provider to get medical help.
